### Mobile App Issues

**Problem:** Permissions not working (Android)

**Solution:**
1. Settings > Apps > Chiron Omega > Permissions
2. Grant all requested permissions
3. For Notification Listener: Settings > Apps > Special Access > Notification Access

---

**Problem:** Firebase connection error

**Solution:**
1. Check `google-services.json` is in `android/app/`
2. Verify Firebase project is active
3. Ensure internet connection
